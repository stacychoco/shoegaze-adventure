Ujubasajuba

Kairon; IRSE!

2014 • 6 songs • 51:03

Genres: Shoegaze

Post-Rock, Space Rock, Progressive Rock

While Ujubasajuba can be strong and overwhelming at times, it does have its calm moments. The riffs in Ujubasajuba are hypnotizing, the vocals are a sweet cherry on top and the saxophone is incorporated well.

This album is psychedelic, atmospheric, energetic and noisy.

How about some shoegazey metal?
